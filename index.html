The Investment Case

Why
Ibeju Lekki
Right Now

Ibeju Lekki is not just another Lagos suburb. It is the site of the most significant infrastructure investment in Nigeria's history. The window to buy before the market fully prices this in is closing fast.

01
🏭
Dangote Refinery

The largest refinery in Africa sits right here in Ibeju Lekki. It employs tens of thousands and drives enormous demand for residential and commercial property.

02
🏗️
Lekki Free Trade Zone

A thriving industrial hub attracting international investment and thousands of workers who all need housing and services nearby.

03
✈️
New International Airport

The Lekki International Airport is in development — set to transform access to this corridor and dramatically increase property demand.

04
📈
Land Appreciation

Land values here have multiplied several times over the past decade and with major infrastructure now operational, the trajectory continues upward.

05
💰
Still Accessible

Compared to Lekki Phase 1 and Victoria Island, prices remain within reach for professionals and diaspora investors — but this window is narrowing.

06
🛣️
Infrastructure Growth

Roads, bridges, utilities and commercial development are rapidly transforming Ibeju Lekki into a fully liveable, investable community.

The Investment Case

Why
Ibeju Lekki
Right Now

Ibeju Lekki is not just another Lagos suburb — it is the site of the most significant infrastructure investment in Nigeria's history. The window to buy before the market fully prices this in is closing fast.
01
🏭
Dangote Refinery

The largest refinery in Africa sits in Ibeju Lekki. It employs tens of thousands and drives enormous demand for residential and commercial property in the corridor.

02
🏗️
Lekki Free Trade Zone

A thriving industrial and commercial hub attracting international manufacturers, logistics companies, and foreign investment — all requiring housing and services.

03
✈️
New International Airport

The Lekki International Airport is in development, set to transform access to the corridor and dramatically increase property demand and valuations.

04
📈
Land Appreciation

Land values in Ibeju Lekki have multiplied several times over the past decade — and with infrastructure now operational, the growth trajectory continues upward.

05
💰
Still Accessible Prices

Compared to Lekki Phase 1 and Victoria Island, prices in Ibeju Lekki remain within reach for professionals and diaspora investors — but this window is narrowing.

06
🛣️
Infrastructure Growth

Roads, bridges, utilities, and commercial development are transforming the area rapidly — making it liveable, investable, and increasingly desirable for families and businesses.
